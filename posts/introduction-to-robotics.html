An Introduction to Robotics

Published on July 6, 2025

Robotics is a multidisciplinary field that involves the design, construction, operation, and use of robots. This introductory post provides an overview of the fundamental concepts in robotics, including kinematics, dynamics, control systems, and perception. We will explore the different types of robots, from industrial arms to autonomous mobile robots, and discuss their applications in various sectors such as manufacturing, healthcare, and exploration. Join us as we uncover the exciting world of robotics and its potential to shape our future.
